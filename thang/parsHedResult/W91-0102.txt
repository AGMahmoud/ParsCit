<abstract confidence="0.990958285714286">In this document, we present a formalism for natural language processing which associates type construction principles to constraint logic programming. We show that it provides more uniform, expressive and efficient tools for parsing and generating language. Next, we present two abstract machines which enable us to design, in a symmetric way, a parser and a generator from that formalism. This abstract machinery is then exemplified by a detailed study of secondary predication within the framework of a principledbased description of language: Government and Binding theory</abstract>
